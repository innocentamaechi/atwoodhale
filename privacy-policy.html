Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Atwood & Hale ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

When you submit the enquiry form on our Contact page, we collect the information you provide directly, which may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • WhatsApp number
  • Business name
  • Website or Facebook page URL
  • Details about your project

When you visit this site, certain technical information may also be collected automatically by advertising platforms we use, as described below.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use tracking technologies from Meta (Facebook) and Google to measure the performance of our advertising and to show relevant ads to people who have visited this site. These tools may place cookies in your browser and collect information such as your IP address, device type, pages viewed, and actions taken on this site (such as submitting the contact form).

  • Meta Pixel: Used to measure the effectiveness of ads run on Facebook and Instagram, and to show our ads to people who have visited this site or match a similar audience profile.
  • Google Ads / Analytics: Used to measure ad performance, understand site traffic, and show relevant ads across Google's network.

These platforms may combine information collected from this site with other data they hold about you as part of their own services. We do not control how Meta or Google use this data beyond our own advertising purposes — you can review Meta's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy directly, and manage your ad preferences through Meta Ad Preferences or Google Ad Settings.

You can also disable cookies generally through your browser settings, though doing so may affect how some parts of this site function.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the enquiry form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and discuss your project
  • Prepare a relevant proposal or landing page review
  • Follow up via email or WhatsApp regarding your enquiry

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. Aside from the advertising platforms described above, information you submit through the contact form is not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Information submitted through the contact form is retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry and, if you become a client, for the duration of our working relationship and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. You may request deletion at any time as described under "Your Rights" below.

Children's Privacy

This website and our services are directed at business owners and decision-makers, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

WhatsApp Redirection

After submitting the contact form, you will be redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message summarizing your enquiry. This message is only sent if you choose to press send within WhatsApp — submitting the form does not automatically send a WhatsApp message on your behalf.

Data Storage

Form submissions are currently handled through the mechanism described in this site's technical documentation (see README.md) and are intended to be connected to a secure backend or third-party form service. No payment information is collected through this website.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us by emailing hello@atwoodhale.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@atwoodhale.com or via WhatsApp.
