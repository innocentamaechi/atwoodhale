Why Good Businesses Lose Good Customers

Your business isn't losing customers to competitors. It's losing them to confusion.

Messaging that talks about you, not the customer

Visitors land on a page full of company history and service lists, but no answer to the one question they actually have: "can this solve my problem?"

Websites that require effort to understand

If a visitor has to scroll, click, and guess to figure out what you do and whether it's for them, most of them leave before finding out.

No visible reason to trust you

No clear credentials, no proof of process, no sense of who's behind the business. Trust isn't assumed online — it has to be built into the page.

No clear next step

A page with five links, three phone numbers, and no single call to action leaves the decision to the visitor. Most won't make one — they'll just leave.

The Fix

A landing page built to do one job: get you the enquiry.

A general business website has to serve everyone — new visitors, existing customers, job seekers, search engines. A landing page has one visitor, one message, and one outcome in mind. That focus is what makes it convert.

01

One message, clearly stated

The page opens by naming the visitor's problem and your solution in plain language — no jargon, no guessing required.

02

Trust built into the structure

Credentials, process, and clarity about who they're dealing with are placed exactly where doubt would naturally appear.

03

One call to action, repeated with intent

Every section moves toward a single next step — a form, a WhatsApp message — instead of splitting attention across competing links.

Questions

Answers before you have to ask.

How long does it take to build a landing page?

Most single landing pages are completed within 5–10 working days from when copy direction and any required assets are confirmed. Full sales funnels and multi-page websites take longer — you'll get a specific timeline after the free review.

I already have a website. Do I need a separate landing page?

Usually, yes. A general website has to serve many purposes at once, which dilutes its ability to convert any single type of visitor. A landing page is built for one traffic source and one action — which is why it typically converts at a higher rate.

How much does this cost?

Pricing depends on scope — a single landing page, a full funnel, and a business website are priced differently. You'll get a clear, specific quote after a short conversation about your goals, not a generic price list.

Do you write the copy, or do I need to provide it?

Copy is included. You'll be asked structured questions about your business, offer, and customers, and the page will be written from that — you're welcome to provide existing copy as a starting point if you prefer.

What if the page doesn't convert as expected?

No one can guarantee a specific conversion rate — anyone who does is not being straight with you. What's guaranteed is a page built on proven structure and clear messaging, with the option to review and adjust based on real performance once it's live.

Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?

Yes. The process is fully remote and works well with clients across Africa and internationally — communication happens over WhatsApp, email, and video call as needed.

What do you need from me to get started?

A short description of your business and offer, your target customer, and any existing branding or content you'd like used. Everything else is covered during the free landing page review.
