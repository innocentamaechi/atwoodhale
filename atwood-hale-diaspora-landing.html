Before You Reach Out

Common questions from diaspora buyers

How do I know this isn't another diaspora scam?

Every property we present comes with an independent title verification report and a live video walkthrough — you see the actual land and the actual paperwork before you commit any funds. We verify before we recommend, not after.

What if I can't travel to Nigeria before buying?

Most of our diaspora clients never set foot on the property before closing. Verification, walkthroughs, and documentation are all handled remotely, with you reviewing everything on video calls at a time that suits your timezone.

What documents will I actually receive?

Depending on the property, you'll receive the verified title documentation (Certificate of Occupancy or Governor's Consent), the survey plan, and your registered contract of sale — all provided digitally and couriered where required.

Which locations do you cover?

We currently focus on Lagos, Abuja and Ibadan, where our verification team has direct relationships with the land registries and can physically inspect every property.

Is there a minimum budget?

Tell us your budget on the call — we work across a range of price points and will only shortlist properties that genuinely fit what you have to invest.
